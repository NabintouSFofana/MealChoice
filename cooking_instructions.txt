
Cooking instructions

Step 1

Wash your vegetables with salt and water.
Slice the vegetables and put them on the side.
Wash your chicken with salt and water also.

Step 2

Season the chicken with salt, paprika and black pepper.
Heat the oil in a 12-inch skillet over medium-high heat.
Add the chicken and cook for 6 minutes or until browned.
Remove the chicken from the skillet.

Step 3

Add 1/4 cup of water, the onion, tomato, garlic in the skillet and cook for 5 minutes.
Stir and add 1 1/2 cups water, let everything cook for 6 minutes, then add the chicken.
Add salt/pepper and cook for 2 minutes. Stir in the rice and reduce the heat to low.
Cover and cook for 5 minutes or until the chicken is done and the rice is tender.
